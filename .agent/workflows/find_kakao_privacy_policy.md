---
description: Finding Privacy Policy Setting in Kakao Developers
---
1. Go to [Kakao Developers Console](https://developers.kakao.com/console/app).
2. Select your app.
3. In the left sidebar, click on **App Settings (앱 설정)** -> **General (일반)**.
4. On the main page content, scroll down to the **Basic Information (기본 정보)** section.
5. Provide a visual guide or alternative navigation if the section is collapsed or named differently.
